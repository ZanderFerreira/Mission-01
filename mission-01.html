New Zealand climbed one spots to number 3 on the world ranking after their big win against Fiji.


New Zealand vs Fiji

Snapdragon Stadium, United States 19/07/2024

Score: NZ 47 : 5 Fiji

New Zealand put on a defensive master class, with Fiji only managing to score one try. New Zealand looks like getting back to previous best form, with the youngsters performing well.


South Africa vs Portugal

Toyota Stadium, South Africa 20/07/2024

Score: RSA 64 : 21 Portugal

South Africa had an electric attacking performance, Scoring ten tries, with Mapimpi scoring a hat-trick. While Dos Santos cut thru the Springbok defence scoring a brace of tries.
